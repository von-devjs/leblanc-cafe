Our Story

From a small cookie-inspired milk tea stand to your favorite dessert beverage destination.

The Leblanc Cafe Journey

Founded in 2023, Leblanc began with a simple vision: to create the perfect cookies and cream milk tea experience. Our founder, Emily Chen, combined her passion for baking and love for milk tea to create our signature recipes.

What started as a small kiosk has grown into multiple locations across the city, but our commitment to quality and flavor remains unchanged. Every drink is handcrafted with premium ingredients and made fresh to order.

We take pride in sourcing the finest tea leaves, using real cookies in our recipes, and creating each drink with care and attention to detail. Our mission is to bring joy through every sip of our delicious creations.
